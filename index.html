JOSE RIZAL

Pride of the Malay Race

HEIGHT: approx. 5'1"

BORN: June 19, 1861

DIED: Dec 30, 1896

PROFESSION: Opthalmologist

EDUCATION: Ateneo, UST, Madrid

Dapitan Engineering

While in exile, he built a water system for the community and established a school without charging the students tuition.

The Scientific Discovery

He discovered specimens in Dapitan named after him: Draco rizali (a flying lizard) and Rachophorous rizali (a frog).

Lothario or Romantic?

He was linked to at least 9 women globally, but his final "wife" was Josephine Bracken, whom he called his 'dulce extranjera'.

Ambidextrous Genius

He could write with both hands at the same time, writing different things with each hand!

TRIVIA: Rizal was a master fencer and trained in the Sala de Armas in Spain. He once nearly had a duel with Antonio Luna over a woman (Nelly Boustead)!

— “He saw death as a noble rest for himself and a sacrifice for his country.”
